Portland Art Studios

A collective of four artist studio buildings · Portland, OR

Portland Art Studios is a collective of four historic properties that together house over 200 artists across Portland's NW Industrial area. Managed by the Unkeles Group, each building has its own character and community while sharing a commitment to affordable, long-term studio space for working artists.
