Alberta Street Gallery

Member-run gallery · Alberta Arts District · Portland, OR

Alberta Street Gallery is a member-run cooperative gallery on NE Alberta Street in Portland's Alberta Arts District. It showcases original work by 30 member artists working across painting, printmaking, photography, fiber, ceramics, and mixed media.

The gallery hosts regular exhibitions, artist receptions, and the annual 6×6 Community Art Show — a beloved event where hundreds of small works by members and the broader community are shown and sold. Members rotate gallery sitting duties, keeping the space community-operated and artist-centered.
