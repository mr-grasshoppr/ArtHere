PAM Rental Sales Gallery

Downtown Portland · Portland Art Museum

The Rental Sales Gallery is part of the Portland Art Museum. Founded in 1959, it is a nonprofit gallery representing nearly 200 artists from Oregon and Washington. The gallery holds over 1,000 works spanning painting, sculpture, drawing, collage, photography, printmaking, and more — all available to rent or purchase directly from the artists.

The gallery hosts at least two major shows per year, including Spring and Fall Member Artists Shows highlighting new work, and periodic New Member Artist Shows open to submissions.
