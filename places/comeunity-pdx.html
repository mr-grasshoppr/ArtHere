ComeUnity PDX

Neighborhood retail, gallery & event space · Portland, OR
"Alone we can do so little; together we can do so much." — Helen Keller

ComeUnity PDX is a purpose-driven business founded by Nell Bostwick, focused on spreading pride in Portland's neighborhoods. It's a space to come together for art and shopping, for the benefit of the community.

The shop features neighborhood-centric gear designed and printed in Portland, an Artist in House program that brings local artists into an approachable, family-friendly gallery space, and an event space for community gatherings. A majority of styles benefit the Oregon Food Bank.
