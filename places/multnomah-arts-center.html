Multnomah Arts Center

Community Arts Center · Multnomah Village · Portland, OR

The Multnomah Arts Center (MAC) is one of the largest comprehensive community arts centers in the nation, offering year-round programs in music, dance, theater, ceramics, painting, printmaking, photography, woodshop, and more. Financial assistance is available to all.

Located in the heart of Multnomah Village, MAC annually serves more than 6,000 students of all ages. It has been recognized by the National Guild of Community Schools of Art, the Regional Arts and Cultural Council, the Oregon Arts Commission, and the National Endowment for the Arts.
