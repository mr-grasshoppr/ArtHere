Kurtis Piltz

Ceramicist · Multnomah Village · Portland, OR

Kurtis Piltz studied ceramics at Portland State University and Portland Community College and has been teaching at the Multnomah Arts Center since it opened. He currently teaches hand-building and wheel throwing at MAC and the Catlin Gabel Community Arts Program.

Contact Kurtis directly through Instagram for a viewing and to purchase work.
