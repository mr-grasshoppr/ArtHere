Jennifer Eaton White

Painter · watercolor, ink, oil, encaustics · Vancouver, WA

Jennifer Eaton White is a Vancouver, Washington-based painter working in watercolor, ink, oil, and encaustics. Her landscapes of the Pacific Northwest coast are spare and quietly observed, holding light and weather rather than describing them.

Her work has been shown at Smith & Vallee Gallery in Edison, Washington and Alberta Street Gallery in Portland.
