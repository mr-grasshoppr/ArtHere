Portland Community Survey

Art Here is a community initiative that celebrates and highlights local artists, connecting neighbors to the artists around them and helping artists find the buyers, collaborators, and opportunities they need to thrive.

We’re piloting in Portland in 2026 and we’d love to hear from you — whether you’re an artist, a neighbor, or just curious. Your answers will help us understand how Art Here can best show up in Portland.

🎁 Complete the survey for a chance to win a $25 gift card — and as part of the survey, you can express interest in being a featured artist when Art Here launches.

Your responses support the arts in Portland. Any quotes or data shared with our partners will not be tied to your name. You can respond anonymously if you prefer not to share your email.
