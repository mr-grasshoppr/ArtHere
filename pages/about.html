About Art Here

Art Here makes local creative communities visible and economically connected. We work city by city, listening first, then building tools and gatherings that help artists find each other, find work, and find the people who already love their work.

Each city instance of Art Here has its own visual identity, designed through a local flyer competition, and its own living directory of artists, places, and opportunities. Underneath the surface, every city shares the same simple structure: who is here, where they make work, what is happening, and how it all connects.

The project is currently piloting in Portland, Oregon, with launches planned in Biloxi, Mississippi and San Jose, California.

Art Here is a project of Art Experience Lab.
