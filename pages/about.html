Art Here puts local artists on the map.

Art Here is a community initiative that makes local artists visible and connects them to economic opportunity. We partner with local organizations to host artist celebrations, capture artist stories, and build a living online directory that helps residents, visitors, and funders find and support local creatives.

We are piloting in Portland during the summer of 2026, with a presence at the Multnomah Days Festival in partnership with Multnomah Arts Center. Our next stops include Biloxi, MS and San Jose, CA.
