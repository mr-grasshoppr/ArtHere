Yong Hong Zhong

Watercolor Painter
Portland, OR
"Painting is my addiction. It is something that I am naturally drawn to and as essential and simple as breathing to me."

Yong Hong Zhong was born in Canton, China and immigrated to the United States when he was twelve years old. He studied art in NYC and worked in several animation studios before returning to his love of painting and relocating with his family to Portland, where he paints and teaches watercolor classes.

Learn more about Yong Hong Zhong and purchase his paintings through his website.
